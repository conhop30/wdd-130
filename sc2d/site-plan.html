Game Overview

Knowledge Is Power

Knowing what a card does and how it does it is everything. When it needs to happen at RTS pace, it's even more crucial to know at a glance. SC2D cards will provide all of that information in a clean way, allowing for you to focus on how to best beat your opponent rather than beating card functionality.

Your battleground: Traditional card games allow for a full view of the battlegroud... Not in SC2D. You'll have to manage the Fog of War by pushing units into danger to earn that turning point of information.
