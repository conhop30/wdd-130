Content

Home page

This page will be the first thing the audience sees, so it is crucial that it is engaging, simple, and shows why they should be interested in investing time into exploring the game.

Images for the Home page

Game Overview

This page will host a sort of "hook tutorial", which introduces why this game is unique and fun due to exclusive mechanics, but not doing so in a way that is too descriptive or long-winded, as to avoid deterring the reader.

Images for the Page 2

Card Gallery

This page will host the entirety of the existing cards in the game, allowing for direct exploration of all of the possibilities and potential interactions to brew. Seeing the cards themselves is crucial to deciding if the audience wants to even entertain the idea of trying out a new card game, so this page will make it possible to establish that information.
